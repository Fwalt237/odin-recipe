Eru

Description

Originated from the South-West of Cameroon, the Eru recipe is a fairly organic and super delicious dish that will delight everyone. The Eru recipe is a very popular dish in Cameroonian cuisine, it is simple to make and above all tasty.

Discover the Eru recipe step by step and in a simplified way to delight your taste buds.

Quantity: for 6 people

Preparation: 30 minutes

Cooking: 01 hour and 15 minutes

Total time: 01 hour and 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of Eru leaves or 3 cups of Eru (or Okok) leaves
  • 750 g spinach leaves or 3 cups water leaves
  • 1 cup of Mandjanga or crayfish
  • Smoked fish (skewers, bifagas or cod)
  • Beef skin
  • 1 kg of beef
  • 1/4 liter of palm oil
  • Salt
  • Chili pepper
  • 200 ml of water: preferably use the cooking juices from the meat and fish
Nota bene: Eru (Okok) leaves are difficult to cook, they must be soaked for 3 to 6 hours beforehand. You can also soak the leaves (Eru) for 1 hour of time and boil them with bicarbonate for 30 minutes.

For one serving of Eru (or okok), use one serving of spinach or waterleaf. Also Spinach is different from waterleaf and for those who cannot easily find waterleaf, spinach is a good substitute.

Steps

  1. Wash and cut all your vegetables
  2. After cutting and washing the beef skin, boil it for 30 minutes then add the previously cleaned and cut beef. Don't forget to add salt
  3. Let it boil for 10 minutes until the meat juices form. Add chili pepper to it. Add the previously soaked fish at the end of cooking. You must keep the juice, it will be useful for the rest of the cooking
  4. In a pot, add the spinach or waterleaf. After 10 minutes, add the Eru and leave the mixture to simmer. Stir occasionally
  5. Add the meat and fish, stir the mixture and simmer for 5 minutes
  6. Then add the cooking juices and palm oil, stir and simmer for 15 minutes
  7. Finally add your crayfish and let simmer for 5 to 10 minutes.

Additional Notes: It can be eaten with Water fufu or Garri.

And.... Here we go. Bon Appetit!
