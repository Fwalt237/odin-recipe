Ndole

Description

Ndole is a dish that has its origins in the Sawa people, at a coastal region in Cameroon. It is almost unthinkable to go to a party or a big ceremony in Cameroon without finding a delicious Ndole recipe. It is a dish that remains very popular in Cameroon.

Discover the Ndole recipe step by step and in a simplified way to delight your taste buds.

Quantity: for 6 people

Preparation: 40 minutes

Cooking: 60 minutes

Total time: 01 hour and 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 450 g of washed ndole balls
  • 500 g white peanuts
  • 2 large onions
  • 400 g smoked cod
  • 1kg of beef
  • Seasoning cube
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. For Ndole to be well cooked, it should be cooked in boiling water with cooking bicarbonate. This method will also allow it to keep its color
  2. Cook your white-skinned peanuts for about 7 minutes
  3. Remove them from the pot, drain them and mash them
  4. Cut your onions into strips
  5. Brown your crushed peanut and crushed onion, add your washed smoked cod, salted beforehand to your liking. You can add cube if you consume it
  6. Add your beef prepared in advance
  7. Let simmer for 15 minutes
  8. Then add your balls of well-mixed ndole
  9. Let everything cook over medium heat for 15 minutes maximum
  10. To finish, cut your onions into strips
  11. Heat your oil to a suitable temperature
  12. Brown your onions for a few seconds maximum and pour everything over your ndole.

Additional Notes: You can enjoy it with rice, steamed tubers (plantains, cassava, yam), or with corn couscous, cassava stick or miondo.

And....That's it. Bon Appetit!
