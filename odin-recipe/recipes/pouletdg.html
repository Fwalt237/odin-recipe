Poulet DG

Description

The "Poulet DG" (Directeur Général)[the pronunciation it's in french, sorry y'all :)] Recipe is an essential dish of Cameroonian gastronomy. This dish is a real delicacy which is always an unanimous favorite on our tables and which remains a reference in Cameroonian cuisine.

Discover the Poulet DG recipe step by step and in a simplified way to delight your taste buds.

Quantity: for 6 people

Preparation: 45 minutes

Cooking: 20 minutes

Total time: 01 hour and 05 minutes

Ingredients

  • Ripe plantain
  • 1 chicken
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 1 Green pepper
  • 1 leek
  • 2 large carrots
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt
  • 6 to 7 sticks of green beans
  • White pepper
  • Chili Pepper

Steps

  1. Clean the chicken and cut it into pieces
  2. Immerse it in salt water to further clean (wash) it. Drain and reserve
  3. Next, cut the plantains into (rings or sticks), lightly salt them and fry them (in the fryer) until they are golden brown. To book
  4. Prepare the vegetables. Cut the onions, carrots, garlic, leek, green beans. To book
  5. Next, wash, seed and cut the peppers into thick wedges. Wash and cut the fruit tomatoes into very small pieces
  6. In a saucepan, heat the oil to brown the chicken pieces on all sides. So book them
  7. Mix in your saucepan, onions, carrots, garlic, green pepper, leek, green beans, tomato. salt and chili pepper
  8. Add the well-seasoned chicken pieces. Stir for a few minutes to mix the vegetables well with the sauce
  9. Finally add the fried plantain slices. Mix gently so as not to crumble them (for approximately 2 minutes)
  10. Simmer for one to 3 minutes. Then turn off the heat
  11. Heat your oil to a suitable temperature
  12. Brown your onions for a few seconds maximum and pour everything over your ndole.

And....It's set. Bon Appetit!
